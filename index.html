About Me

Hi. My name is "Sher", and it means "Lion" in my farsi language, not Cher, the singer. I come from Tajikistan, one of the mountainous countries located in the Central Asia.

In 2015, I was offered four year full-tuition scholarship to attend Berea College which is located at Berea, KY. Currently, I am a junior studying computer science. Berea College is a work and study school; every student has a 15 hour labor position related to his/her major each semester. Thus, I work as a Full-Stack Student Software Developer in a team of 8 building web applications that improve the administrative workflow of various Berea College departments. My goal is to make a positive impact in my community and enhance the lives of people using the power of technology.
